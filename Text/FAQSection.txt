FAQ ITEMS (Question — Answer)
------------------------------

Q1: How long does it take to implement Acadhub?
A1: Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on your institution size and requirements.
    Our dedicated onboarding team ensures a smooth transition with minimal disruption.

Q2: Is training provided for staff and teachers?
A2: Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all users including administrators, teachers, and staff.
    Training includes live sessions, video tutorials, and ongoing support.

Q3: Can Acadhub integrate with our existing systems?
A3: Acadhub offers robust API integration capabilities and can connect with most existing educational
    systems, payment gateways, and third-party applications.

Q4: What kind of support do you offer?
A4: We provide 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and chat. Our support team is always ready
    to help with any questions or issues.

Q5: Is my data secure with Acadhub?
A5: Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, maintain regular backups, and comply with all data
    protection regulations. Your data security is our top priority.

Q6: Can I customize Acadhub for my institution?
A6: Yes, Acadhub is highly customizable. We can tailor workflows, reports, and features to match
    your specific institutional requirements.
